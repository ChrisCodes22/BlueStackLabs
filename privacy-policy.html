Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

Overview

Story Template Autosuggest ("the Extension") is developed and maintained by Blue Stack Labs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data or story content.

The Extension operates entirely locally within your browser and does not communicate with any external servers except for:

  • Loading template definitions from our public GitHub repository
  • One-time license key validation (license key only, no other data)

Information We Store Locally

The following information is stored only in your browser's local storage and never leaves your computer:

  • License Key: Your unique license key for product activation
  • Last Validation Date: Timestamp of last license check (for periodic re-validation)

Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Story content you write or insert
  • NetSuite account information
  • Customer data or work order details
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Browser history or browsing data
  • Personal information of any kind

Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • activeTab: To detect when you're working in NetSuite and provide template suggestions
  • storage: To save your license key locally in your browser

These permissions are used solely for the Extension's core functionality and never for data collection.

Third-Party Services

The Extension uses the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Templates are loaded from a public repository. No user data is transmitted.
  • Stripe (for payments): When you purchase a license, Stripe processes payment. See Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy

License Key Validation

Your license key is validated locally within the Extension using cryptographic methods. The key contains only: a user identifier and expiration date. It contains no personal information.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is designed for professional use by adults in a workplace setting. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect your data, there is no data to request, delete, or modify. Your information stays on your device.

If you wish to remove your license key from your browser:

  1. Open Chrome Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to Application → Local Storage
  3. Find and delete items starting with "stl_"

Alternatively, uninstalling the Extension removes all stored data.
